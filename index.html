Fibrillated PP Yarn

  • Custom tensile strength braid reinforcement for durable use.
  • Flexible, abrasion-resistant wrapping suitable for high-speed braiding lines.
  • Resistant to chemicals and UV for long-term outdoor storage and field use.
  • Maintains flexibility in low-temperature environments.
  • Available in customised spool configurations.
  • Precisely wound for optimal handling and consistent feed.

Slitted BOPP/Polyester Films

  • Spiral-wrapped protective layer for strength and integrity.
  • Moisture-resistant barrier against humidity and ingress.
  • Intermediate jacket between braid and sheath.
  • Maintains dimensional stability for uniform velocity.
  • Precise slit widths for minimal waste.
  • Antistatic properties for ESD prevention.
  • Stable static-control across conditions.
  • Reduces dust attraction during processing.

Supreme Laminations

Supreme Lamination was established under the leadership of Mr. Ajeet Lal with the objective of manufacturing and supplying reliable industrial components that meet the specific needs of customers across India. The company began its journey with a modest setup and a focused product range, operating with the principle that consistent quality and timely delivery are the keys to building trust in the market.

From the initial stages, Mr. Ajeet Lal has been personally involved in all aspects of the business, from evaluating raw material quality, supervising manufacturing processes, and ensuring precision in moulding, to managing logistics and responding to urgent customer requirements. This hands-on management style has allowed the company to maintain strict control over quality and delivery schedules, even in challenging circumstances.

Supreme Lamination operates with advanced injection moulding and blow moulding machines, enabling the production of a wide variety of precision components. The company’s product range serves multiple industrial sectors, where durability, dimensional accuracy, and consistent performance are essential. Every product is subject to quality checks at various stages, ensuring compliance with customer specifications and industry standards.

Looking ahead, Supreme Lamination aims to strengthen its manufacturing capabilities, explore new product lines, and expand its reach to serve a wider market. While the company continues to adapt to changing market demands, it remains committed to the three core principles laid down by Mr. Ajeet Lal: integrity, reliability, and delivering products that meet or exceed customer expectations.
